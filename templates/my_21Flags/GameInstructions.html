Welcome to the 21 Flags game, Thai 21 or a version of the Nim game. The game is rather simple: There are 21 flags, which each player will remove during their turn. You must select one, two, or three flags to remove during each turn, with the objective of picking up the last flag. You will be alternating turns with the computer, who shares the same objective. The player to pick up the last flag will be the winner.

You will be playing 15 rounds of this game.
